About Me

My name is Mostafa H. Deldoost. I am an interdisciplinary researcher in neuroeconomics and behavioral economics. I am passionate about understanding what happens in the brain when we make decisions. I use EEG and eye-tracking techniques to connect neural activity with economic behavior, exploring how humans think and choose.
